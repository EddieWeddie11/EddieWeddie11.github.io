    • What are the role of values, empathy, and self-awareness in learning and programming?

  • When someone is pitching a product that they want you to design, we have to realise that product is valuable to them and though we may not share the same perspective you have to respect their vision
  • Empathy is important as if you’re not able to relate or understand their product pitch, it will make it difficult to feel as if you’re making progress. Trying to put yourself in their shoes is important
  • Self awareness is important as well because at times programmers might be working to a tight deadline and sure getting the work done for your client or boss is important but if you’re sleep deprived and not getting enough nutrients in, there’s going to be long term consequence
